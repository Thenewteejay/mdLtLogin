About Us

Welcome to Lifetech OCMS. The leading CMS platform for web development

Our story started in 2016 when Ajayi Abolore, the founder of Lifetech OCMS, was triggered by the rate at which websites were being blocklisted or hijacked by hackers. He decided to embark on developing a framework that allows developers to construct secured websites after series of researches and testing had been carried out at Ogun State Institute of Technology (OGITECH), Igbesa. This project birthed Lifetech OCMS, which became popular immediately after the release of its first version in 2021. Lifetech OCMS is a platform made for developers to build unlimited websites easily, quickly and efficiently
